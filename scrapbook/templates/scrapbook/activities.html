Tell us a Story

Write, Speak, Move

Pick one of your pages and tell its story. It could be a treasured memory or pure daft fiction, whatever strikes your fancy.
Tell a friend, write it down, record it or play the song from the page and move to the music.

Historical Map

Explore the historical map.

Can you find any important places? Where you grew up? Met someone important?

Nature Watch

From your window, or from a wander outside see how many different species of animal you can find.

Some things you can do:
  • Take photographs
  • Sketch your favourite (or describe it to someone for them to try and copy)
  • Collect some souvenirs (bright leaves)

Getting Colourful

Release Your Inner Artist

Take the title of one of your scrapbook pages and create a piece of art based on it.
You could use pen, pencil, crayon, paint, whatever you have around.
How you do it is up to you. Sketch shapes with pencil or get your hands messy and focus on playing with paint.
